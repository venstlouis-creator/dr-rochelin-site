Effective date: January 1, 2026

About our text messages

Dr. Rochelin Aesthetic & Wellness offers text (SMS) messaging so we can reach you about appointment reminders, scheduling, follow-up, and important updates about your care. The program is a convenience, and it is always optional.

Your consent

By giving us your mobile number and agreeing to receive texts, you consent to receive SMS messages from our office at that number, including messages sent using automated technology. Consent to receive texts is not a condition of receiving care from us. You can ask us to reach you another way at any time.

Message frequency and cost

Message frequency varies based on your appointments and care. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile plan. We do not charge for the messages themselves, but your carrier's standard rates still apply.

How to stop messages

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text from us. After you do, we will send one message confirming you have been unsubscribed, and you will not receive further texts unless you sign up again. You can also call (305) 974-5548 and ask us to remove your number.

How to get help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or call our office at (305) 974-5548. For questions about your mobile plan or charges, contact your wireless carrier.

Privacy

We treat any information shared by text with the same care described in our Notice of Privacy Practices. Please avoid sending sensitive medical details by text, since standard SMS is not a secure channel. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Questions about texting? Call Dr. Rochelin Aesthetic & Wellness at (305) 974-5548, or visit us at 13571 Biscayne Blvd, North Miami Beach, FL 33181. See also our Notice of Privacy Practices.
